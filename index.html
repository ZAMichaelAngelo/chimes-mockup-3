Who We Are

Owner-managed.
Operator trusted.

Founded in 2003 by Paul Chimes and Elias Nyathi, Chimes Crane Hire brings over two decades of lifting expertise to every project. Before Chimes, the founders ran the highly successful Beacon Crane Hire through the 1990s.

Today, we operate from our Germiston yard in Johannesburg with a 90% Tadano fleet and a team of certified operators who live by our founding values.
